PARKING INFORMATION
There is metered parking on 52nd Street available after 6pm Monday through Friday and all day on Saturday. Sunday is free. The meter costs $ 2.00 per hour and is free after midnight. The meters accept quarters and subway tokens and can be fed for 6hours at a time.

There is discounted parking for Bombay Palace available at Meyers Parking Garage diagonally opposite the restaurant and the rate is a flat $25.00 for the evening up to 1 a.m. Get your parking ticket stamped at the restaurant.
