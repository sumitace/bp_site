From: FROM NEW JERSEY
Take the New Jersey Turnpike to Lincoln Tunnel. From Lincoln Tunnel take 42nd Street East. Drive till you reach 6th Avenue (a.k.a. Avenue of the Americas). Turn left on 6th Avenue and continue north till 52 street. Turn right on 52 street and you will see Bombay Palace on your right about halfway down the block.

From: FROM LONG ISLAND
Take the Long Island Expressway (495 west) to Queensborough bridge upper level. Turn left on 2nd Avenue South and drive till 51st street. Turn right on 51st Street and continue West till 6th Avenue (a.k.a. Avenue of the Americas). Turn right on 6th Avenue and drive north one block to 52nd Street. Turn right on 52nd Street and you will see Bombay Palace on your right about halfway down the block.

From: FROM CONNECTICUT & UPSTATE NEW YORK
Take I 95 South to I 287 West. To Hutchinson Parkway South. ToCross Bronx Expressway West. To Henry Hudson Parkway South. To West Side Highway (South). To 56th Street Exit. Go East on 56th Street to 7th Avenue. Turn right on 7th Avenue and continue to 52nd Street. Turn left on 52nd Street. Cross 6th Avenue (a.k.a. Avenue of the Americas) and you will see Bombay Palace on your right halfway down the block between 5 & 6th Avenues.
